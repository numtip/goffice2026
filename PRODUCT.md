# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Primary users are Green Office staff, assessors, executives, and auditors who need to inspect Maejo University's Green Office readiness, resource indicators, category criteria, and evidence trail from a static bilingual website.

## Product Purpose

GOFFICE2026 is a static-first Green Office platform for showing, measuring, proving, and improving certification readiness. It prioritizes dashboard visibility, category and indicator traceability, and evidence discoverability without adding backend complexity.

## Positioning

A bilingual, evidence-first Green Office control surface where every public claim can be traced to categories, indicators, dashboards, or supporting documents.

## Brand Personality

Calm, accountable, institutional. The interface should feel trustworthy and practical, with public-facing clarity and auditor-grade traceability.

## Anti-references

Avoid CMS/admin complexity, decorative dashboards, generic SaaS landing-page tropes, glass-heavy AI templates, unexplained jargon, and any design move that hides evidence lineage.

## Design Principles

- Reuse before build: use existing components, data, and documented architecture before adding new surfaces.
- Static first: keep content, taxonomy, dashboards, and evidence in repo-native files unless an approved ADR changes scope.
- Evidence first: make category, indicator, document, and provenance links visible with minimal clicks.
- Performance first: preserve a small static site with minimal client-side JavaScript.
- Maintainability first: prefer clear, repeated patterns over one-off visual inventions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for public and internal preview use. Keyboard navigation, semantic structure, readable contrast, bilingual text expansion, reduced motion, and non-color-only status indicators are required design constraints.
